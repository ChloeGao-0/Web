Summary

The Percy Jackson And The Olympians is a book series written by Rick Riordan. It is a 5 book series, telling of a young boy named Percy Jackson. He finds out that he is a demigod (meaning one of his parent is a human while the other parent is a god or a goddess) and is then brought to Camp-Blood. Through each of the books, he goes on a quest/ adventure with different purposes.

The Five Books

There are five books in the Percy Jackson and The Olympians series. The first book called "The Lighting Thief" tells of Percy finding that he is a demigod and is the son of Poseidon. He goes on a quest to find the thief who stole the lightning bolt of Zeus. The second book called "The Sea of Monsters" is about Percy going on a journey to find the Golden Fleece
